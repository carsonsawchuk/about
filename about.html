What is Formula SAE Electric?

Formula SAE Electric is an international engineering design competition that provides students with the unique opportunity to enhance their engineering design and project management skills through practical application. We create a formula-style, single-seat race car over the course of a school year in order to participate in FSAE Electric. The competition is comprised of dynamic events to test the vehicleâ€™s performance and reliability and static events to test the rigor and feasibility of the engineering design and business strategy. The competition pushes the boundaries of conventional learning, pushing students to develop skills applicable to the professional world that are overlooked in traditional school curriculum.

Formula Subgroups

To divide work and allow members to work within their unique discipline, Formula has multiple subgroups.

Areo Aero is responsible for the areodynamic performance of the car. This involves extensive analysis and design of elements that create downforce and sideforce. Analysis of cooling the motor and other components is involved as well.

Battery System Batteries systems is responsible for configuring the battery and managing the thermals when under load.

Business The business team prepares a presentation on the car and gives it during the static events of the competition.

Chassis Chassis is responsible for designing and manufacturing the frame. This involves creating a platform that can bring together other subgroups such as suspension, areodynamics, and powertrain.

Driver Controls Driver controls design and put together the components that the driver will interact with when driving. This includes, steering, ergonomics, and pedal systems.

Drive Train Drivertrain is responsible for getting the power from the electric motor to the wheels efficiently.

Suspension Suspension is responsible for providing a comfortable ride while the main objective is to maximize performance around turns. This is done by keeping the bottom interfaced with the ground effectively.
